The Collections

From the elemental clarity of the Perpetuelle to the celestial complexity of the Grande Complication, every Aurum Tempus collection is built around a single philosophical idea — and executed without compromise.

Classic Collection

Perpetuelle I

The original expression of the Vallat philosophy. A 40mm rose-gold case housing the Calibre AT-1889 — a movement traced directly from Édouard Vallat's 1889 sketches, updated only with silicon escapement components.

  • Case40mm 18K Rose Gold
  • MovementCalibre AT-1889, Manual Wind
  • Power Reserve72 hours
  • Water Resistance30m
  • CrystalSapphire, double AR-coating
  • StrapHand-stitched alligator
USD 8,900
Classic Collection

Élégance Ultra Thin

At 5.9mm thick, the Élégance is the thinnest movement ever produced in-house. A platinum dress watch for those who consider the wristwatch a whisper, not a shout.

  • Case38mm Platinum 950
  • Thickness5.9mm
  • MovementCalibre AT-5900, Ultra Thin
  • Power Reserve48 hours
  • DialCream Grand Feu enamel
USD 12,500
Sport Collection

Chronos Sport

A flyback chronograph born from an Everest expedition brief. Black ceramic-coated titanium case, 300m water resistance. The movement that convinced mountaineer and collector alike.

  • Case44mm Titanium / Black Ceramic
  • MovementCalibre AT-2241, Flyback
  • Power Reserve65 hours
  • Water Resistance300m
  • BraceletTitanium, rubber option
USD 9,800
Grand Complication

Grande Complication

The pinnacle of horological ambition. Perpetual calendar, minute repeater, tourbillon, and moon phase — all within a 42mm platinum case of extraordinary restraint. Twelve produced annually.

  • Case42mm Platinum 950
  • MovementCalibre AT-9600 Gran Compl.
  • ComplicationsTourbillon, Min. Repeater, Perp. Cal.
  • Production12 pieces per year
  • Assembly2,400 hours per piece
USD 24,900
Ladies' Collection

Ladies' Pavé

A 34mm white-gold masterpiece set with 186 VVS diamonds along the bezel and case — totalling 1.8 carats. Powered by the ultra-thin Calibre AT-3400, hand-wound, 50-hour reserve.

  • Case34mm 18K White Gold
  • Diamonds186 × VVS1, 1.8ct total
  • MovementCalibre AT-3400, Manual
  • DialMother-of-pearl
  • StrapWhite alligator / bracelet
USD 14,500
