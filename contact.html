Lifetime Care

Your Timepiece
Deserves a Doctor.

Every Aurum Tempus movement is serviced exclusively at our Geneva manufacture. We do not authorise third-party service — the tolerances of our calibres require the same tools used to build them.

Standard full service intervals are recommended every 7–10 years for manual movements and every 10–12 years for automatic calibres. All services include a full accuracy recertification.

Book a Service

What Your Service Includes

  • ✦Complete disassembly and ultrasonic cleaning
  • ✦Replacement of worn components from original stock
  • ✦Full lubrication with MOEBIUS oils and greases
  • ✦16-day timing and positional regulation
  • ✦Case and bracelet restoration (polishing + gaskets)
  • ✦Updated service certificate and return shipping
